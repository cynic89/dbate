What's new?

We believe people have the right to Agree or Disagree any article or blog. A blog becomes more engaging when the readers are allowed DEBATE about it, and not just talk about it.Commenting should not only be about posting opinions, rather it should also be about expressing your stance on the topic.

Narad aims at providing a DEBATING platform, where users can comment "for" or "against" the topic, or perhaps even be equivocal about it. This creates a more engaging atmosphere for the article, thereby making people spend more time on the blog.
